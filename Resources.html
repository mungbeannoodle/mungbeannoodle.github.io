Mental Health Resources

Crisis Support

These organizations provide care and intervention for queer crises such as depression, suicidal thoughts, and homelessness.

The Trevor Project

The Trevor Project provides resources such as counseling, suicide help, and a 24/7 hotline. They also provide online spaces for community and support groups.

Trans LifeLine

Trans Lifeline provides a 24/7 hotline, as well as resources on legal transitioning, support groups, etc. They also support undocumented and disabled queers.

LGBTQ Hotline

This organization provides 24/7 hotlines for youth and seniors and those just coming out, as well as a general hotline. There are also support chatrooms!

National Runaway Safeline

This organization provides 24/7 hotlines for youth ages 12 to 21. Hotlines can be accessed through email, texts, phone, and online chatrooms.

Therapy

The following resources are safe spaces that are specifically made to support queer mental health.

Pride Counseling

Provides discreet, online therapy specifically for queer people.

Talkspace

Provides psychiatry and therapy for individuals, youth, and couples.

Are you queer and unsure of how to approach the medical system? Are you a provider that wants to learn how to provide inclusive care? Check out the resources below!

Why We Need Queer Resources

LGBTQ+ Disparities in Healthcare

  • Queer individuals experience healthcare disparities and poorer health than straight and cisgender individuals do.
  • Healthcare providers lack immediate education and knowledge on how to treat queer people.
  • Many healthcare providers feel unprepared in treating queer patients.
  • There are disparities in treating queer people's mental health.
  • Older queer individuals experience disparities in the healthcare field.

How Queer-Safe Healthcare Helps

  • Telehealth is life-saving for LGBTQ+ individuals.
  • Telehealth helps get rid of inequalities in healthcare.
